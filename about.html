About Us

Catada Gold and Diamonds Trading-Sole Proprietorship LLC was Established in May 2023 by a Professional Engineer known as Azharul Ishaque Mohammed. Having a Passion for Business and Entrepreneurship, he previously held Infinite Waste Services LLC in Dubai. Having Business losses during the Covid-19 era, he sold of the existing Waste Management Service business and starting looking out for business delivering stable and sustainable Income. One of the Potential business identified is Trading of Rough Diamonds and Raw Gold which withstood the Covid-19 phase successfully. Always a promoter of abiding by the laws of the Land, He took up a Wholesale Raw Gold and Diamonds Trading license from the Abu Dhabi Department of Economic Development. Today the Company is registered in four segments, mainly: Wholesale of Unworked Gold and Precious Metals Trading Wholesale of Jewellery Diamonds and Precious Stones Exporting Importing We intend to do our business in an Ethical manner, abiding by the laws of the Land and paying all the relevant Taxes. The Company is expected to make a huge turn over in the months to come.


We start by extending my deepest gratitude to our partners in success: those who deal with Catada Gold and Diamonds; namely the customers, suppliers, workers, and officials. Before getting into details pertaining to the group, we find it appropriate to give a quick review of the group and its companies, its points of strength and challenges encountered through its history, so as to familiarize all who relate to it along with its new activities. We believe the company that neglects earnest and continued self-development, in this fast-pace world is doomed to not survive the cut throat competition. We are since 6 years in the Diamond industry, we have over come various challenges and survived the storm to stand where we are today, successful and every growing.
OUR FUTURE PLANS FOR GROWTH TO NEXT LEVEL Our employees are central to our success and a culture of integrity. With them, we continue to build a company that’s competitive, profitable while contributing to the economic growth and aiming for sustainable operations. The future plans for Catada Gold and Diamonds is to make a world class diamond cutting and polishing facility in United Arab Emirates and India . This move will hope to create many more opportunities for the company and continue our success to expand across the UAE with job opportunities provided through out in line with the vision of UAE’s tremendous inclusive growth.
